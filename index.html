About the project

Τhe Τimles is a potential collection of alternative, interesting articles. The articles of Timeles deal with classic topics such as science and cooking without remaining only in trivial information, but bring to the surface alternative aspects. What is special about timeliness, however, is not only that. You have the opportunity to explore the articles that interest you to choose and experiment with different styles and different periods.It is not just a repository of interesting but already existing articles, it is an aesthetic journey into the history of typography and the respective landmarks, choosing from different dates and seeing characteristic features you can explore, recognize,and remember memorable events.

Documentation

Are you ready to start your journey? You can choose for your favorite article 4 different styles, which will bring you one step closer to exploring your chosen period. Through this function the goal is the possibility of exploring the evolution of typographic design over the centuries. Each topic is designed to include all those features that will lead you to a more plausible preview of how the article would have been published at the chosen given time. Fonts were used for visual realization,margins, spaces, colors, layouts and appearance of content,carefully selected to always direct the timeless experience to the intended goal. The choice of these features was made after a careful overlook of the respective periods and is based on features related to colors, images and trends in general. To see in detail the basis of all our decisions, as well as all the influences we had on the choices of personalizing our styles, see the following document, join the Timeless team.
